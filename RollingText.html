Several baby animals are living in a jungle, where they explore on a daily basis aspects of their lives in their adventures and interactions with their parents and grandparents. At the end of each episode, they come to some fresh understanding about their lives. Sometimes they have to admit the child-world mistakes they make. They learn, for instance, that trusting their parents and telling them the truth from the scratch is always the right thing to do and is the better option compared to hiding facts and lying to them. The collective nature of these experiences, which involves them all, is what makes the animated series a special and lifelike story.
